The story

Tremaine Chappell, better known as SoulTré

Based in Houston and shaped early by '70s soul, jazz and funk, SoulTré built his first beat in GarageBand for a song called "Get Down" — the first track he ever put out publicly. Since then his ear has stretched across R&B, hip-hop and beyond, always led by the source material rather than a single genre.

His catalog spans full projects like Fantastic T, the Hooked On You EP, and the original score for the documentary An Adolescent's State of Mind — plus collaborations with artists like Jae the Lyoness and LIVE 4EVA.
